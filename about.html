SEDS pursues a mission of educating people about the benefits of space, by supporting a network of interested students, by providing an opportunity for members to develop their leadership skills, and inspiring people through our involvement in space-related projects. SEDS believes in a space-faring civilization and that focusing the enthusiasm of young people is the key to our future in space. One of its major objectives is to generate interest and help people in pursuing space oriented careers.

CORE

The core organises events and national as well as international conferences for the enrichment of college students. The agendas cover all of astronomical and aeronautical sciences and engineering. Be it black holes, star trails or rocket engineering, nothing is out of our scope. SINC, i.e, SEDS India National Conference is our flagship annual space conclave.

OUTREACH

The outreach program of SEDS India has been a great success in the affiliated chapters. The main aim of this program is to spread awareness about space among the under privileged childeren in rural India and also an extensive set of events and activities that are designed especially for the childeren residing in orphanages.

PROJECTS

It is the engineering division under SEDS-VIT. It's members and the members of various project groups under SEDS umbrella have worked on projects like Blimp, RC-Flyer, Rubik's cube solver, CanSAT, European Rover Challenge and SpaceX's Hyperloop.
