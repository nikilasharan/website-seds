OUR ACHIEVEMENTS



2014

SEDS VIT Projects was founded by the third year batch of VIT who shared a common interest and love for the exploration of space through technical aspects

2016

The team participated in SpaceX's hyperloop competition. SEDS-VIT Hyperloop team was one of the 115 teams which were selected to present their design in the SpaceX Hyperloop design weekend, held at Texas A&M University from Jan 29 and 30, 2016.

Two of our projects were selected to be presented in the Student Special Symposium(S3) of the Large Scale Multi-Disciplinary Systems of National Significance– Trends and Challenges (LAMSYS) that was held on 24th and 25th of June 2016 in Indian Space Research Organisation’s (ISRO) Satish Dhavan Space Centre, Sriharikota.

Our team of four presented the ERC Rover design at the 19th ISTE National Students Convention 2016, on the 16th of October and were selected as the overall winners of the competition.

2017

The SEDS-VIT CanSat Team were the runners up while competing against 87 teams around the world in the recently held CanSat 2017 competition organised by AIAA and AAS, sponsored by NASA!

Students of SEDS-VIT Secured 2nd place worldwide in ERC(European Rover Challenge)

2018

The team bagged the 7th place in Asia in SUAS(STudent Unmanned Aerial System) 2018. This involved a large amount of hardwork and many sleepless nights accompanied with sweat and heat during the testing phase of the project.

With a large amount of perseverance and determination theteam steadily secured 3rd place in Asia in the famous ERC(European Rover Challenege). The treamendous amount of hardwork finally paid off.
