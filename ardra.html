TEAM ARDRA

Our own SEDS Vit projects dedicates Team Ardra for the sole purpose of creating Unmanned Aerial Vehicles and participates in international competitions all around the world to represent VIT. The AUVSI SUAS Competition is designed to foster interest in Unmanned Aerial Systems (UAS), stimulate interest in UAS technologies and careers, and to engage students in a challenging UAS mission. The construction of the drone involved immense sweat and toil every night for several months. Team Ardra continues to bring great pride to VIT and will hopefully continue to win more accolades in the future and do great justice to the hardwork put into the creation of the drone.
